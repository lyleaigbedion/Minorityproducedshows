The Bulletproof Symbol

Luke Cage speaks to the violence constantly aired on the news of black men and boys being gunned down. The idea of being black and bulletproof is often a fantasy, speaks more to the idea of being black and unharmed by institutionalized racism.

Positive Role Models

What This Means For Minorities

These productions allow us minorities to have positive role models, as they depict aspects of life that concern the minority community. As Zook puts it, these shows allow us “…to inhale just a bit deeper, to reflect a fraction of a dramatic minute longer. Such shows help us to know our fears, desires, and memories are often, collective, not individual.”
